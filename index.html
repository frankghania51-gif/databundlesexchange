HOW TO BECOME A DATA BUNDLE AND AIRTIME VENDOR AND EARN HIGH PROFITS.

Becoming a data retailer or vendor simply means buying data bundle in bulk and reselling in portions to earn profit, it's almost identical to how all telecommunication services buy and resell. In these instances, the customer must activate their sim card as a vendor to be able to access the short code service and the retail app to transfer data. To transfer data or airtime, simply dial the short code, select airtime and data option, enter the recipients number and enter the amount of data to be transferred and select payment option as your retail account, immediately the transfer is complete, your data account will be debited and you will see your remaining data balance. Retailers receive instant 50% additional trade bonus on all purchases, these offers does not expire, from offers such as 1000GB for GHS450 and more, to access the option, click on REGISTER below to start.
